Facilities

Guest Rooms

Valhalla Ski Club offers high quality accommodation only 200m from the Great Alpine Road. Accommodation is ideal for couples, families and individuals; with bedrooms available to sleep 3 â€“ 6 people. Every bedroom comes equipped with en suite (separate toilet from shower) and self contained heaters. During the snow season freshly laundered linen (including pillowcases, bedsheets and towels) are supplied for each bed. Doonas and extra blankets are also provided for each bed.

Extra mattresses can be provided up request prior to arrival.

Every room also has access to a private fridge and pantry cupboard to store food and beverages.

Kitchen and Dining

Valhalla Ski Club provides a full kitchen (ovens, microwaves, stoves) and dining area for self catering, including all cooking utensils and dishes, and dining crockery and cutlery. Breakfast is supplied to all guest including a range of cereals (rice bubbles, corn flakes, vita wheats, oats), bread, milk and a variety of spreads (including butter).

Additionally guests have access to the common pantry which includes a range of sauces, baking ingredients and a variety of herbs and spices. Tea and coffee are also available.

For those who have never been to Mt Hotham before, we suggest that guests bring a majority of their food with them as it can be expensive to shop on the mountain.
