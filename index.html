Located only 200 metres off the Great Alpine Road and an easy walk to Frosty's Drop Ski Run, or Bus Stop 7 for the Village Bus, Valhalla Ski Club offers friendly and comfortable accommodation for members and guests.

Valhalla is a Small Club Lodge founded over 40 years ago as a Co- operative and has 26 members. The priority of the ski lodge is to enable everyone to have a comfortable, social & inexpensive holiday.

Rates are from $80 - $120 per person per night. Room rates are based on occupancy per room. Infants (Children under 5 years old) are free of charge and there is a porta-cot and high chair. You can reserve these at the time of your booking.

To check the availability or to make a booking, click on the book now button and enter intended dates.

Alternatively, you can phone the office between 9.00am to 5.00pm, Monday to Friday or email anytime.

Before you arrive, please familiarise yourself with the rules and lodge information.

Facilities

Valhalla has a spacious lounge room with open fire, games area, and wraparound balconies with breathtaking views. Each of the eight bedrooms has its own ensuite (separate toilet from shower).

Accommodation is ideal for couples, families and individuals; with bedrooms available to sleep 3 – 6 people. Rooms are individual heated for your comfort. During the snow season freshly laundered linen (including pillowcases, bedsheets and towels) are supplied for each bed.

Kitchen and Dining

Every room is allocated a large section in the Kitchen fridge and a pantry cupboard to store food and beverages. The Lodge has a large kitchen (ovens, microwaves, stoves) and dining area for self catering, including all cooking utensils and dishes, and dining crockery and cutlery. Breakfast is supplied which includes a range of cereals (muesli, oats, weetbix etc.), bread, milk and a variety of spreads (including butter).

Additionally guests have access to the common pantry which includes a range of sauces (tomato, bbq), baking ingredients (flour, rice & sugar) and a variety of condiments. Tea and coffee is also provided. We have Plungers, a Nespresso & Expresso Machine.

There is a bunk room where you can book a bed in a shared room, otherwise rooms are sold for exclusive use. Availability and room rates are advertised on our booking site.

If you would like Over Snow Transport for yourself and luggage, you can book with us when you book on line.
